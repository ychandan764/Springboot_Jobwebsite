Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I receive a response?
We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call our support line.
Can I schedule a demo of the platform?
Yes! For recruiters interested in using LinkedOut for their hiring needs, we offer personalized demos. Select "Partnership Opportunity" as your subject.
How do I report a technical issue?
Select "Technical Support" as your subject and provide as much detail as possible about the issue, including screenshots if available.
Do you offer support in other languages?
Currently, our primary support language is English. However, we're working on expanding our support to include Spanish, French, and Mandarin in the near future.
Can I visit your office?
We welcome visitors! Please schedule an appointment in advance by contacting us through this form or calling our office.
